Fluid equations
===============
This section describes the equation terms which are applied to the various fluid
quantities in DREAM. The fluid quantities solved for in DREAM are

+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| Name                    | Description                          |
+=========================+======================================+
| :math:`E_\phi`          | Toroidal electric field              |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`\psi`            | Poloidal magnetic flux               |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`n_{\rm cold}`    | Cold electron density                |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`n_{\rm hot}`     | Hot electron density                 |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`n_{\rm i}^{(j)}` | Ion charge state density             |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`n_{\rm RE}`      | Runaway electron density             |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`T_{\rm cold}`    | Cold electron temperature            |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
| :math:`T_{\rm i}`       | Ion species temperature              |
+-------------------------+--------------------------------------+
